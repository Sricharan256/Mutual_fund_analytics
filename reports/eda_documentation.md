# Mutual Fund Analytics Capstone Project

## Day 3: Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)

### Objective

The objective of Day 3 was to perform Exploratory Data Analysis on mutual fund datasets and generate meaningful insights using visualizations. The analysis focused on NAV trends, AUM growth, SIP inflows, investor demographics, geographic distribution, portfolio allocation, and fund performance metrics.

## Key Insights

### Insight 1

NAV values showed a strong upward trend during the 2023 bull market period.

### Insight 2

Several schemes experienced temporary corrections during 2024 market volatility.

### Insight 3

SBI Mutual Fund maintained the highest Assets Under Management among all fund houses.

### Insight 4

Monthly SIP inflows reached an all-time high of ₹31,002 crore in December 2025.

### Insight 5

Large Cap and Flexi Cap categories attracted the highest investor inflows.

### Insight 6

The age group between 26 and 40 years contributed the majority of investment activity.

### Insight 7

T30 cities contributed a larger share of investments compared to B30 cities.

### Insight 8

Industry folio count increased significantly from 13.26 crore to 26.12 crore during the study period.

### Insight 9

Technology and Financial Services sectors received the highest portfolio allocation.

### Insight 10

Funds with higher risk generally delivered higher long-term returns, demonstrating a positive risk-return relationship.
